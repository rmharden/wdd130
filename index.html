Florida

Florida is a state in the southeastern part of the United States. It is bordered by two states in the north - Georgia and Alabama. To the east is the Atlantic Ocean and on the west is the Gulf of Mexico. The "Sunshine State" has two climate regions - subtropic and tropic. Tallahassee is the capital and is located in the nothwestern part of the state. Other major cities include Orlando, Tampa, and Miami. Central east coast is often refered to as "The Space Coast" where you can enjoy beaches and view many rocket launches.
