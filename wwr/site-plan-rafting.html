Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to invite others to the exciting experience of whitewater rafting and to share information, education, and safety practices to enhance that experience with their friends and family.

Audience

This site strives to target families and their friends to visit areas where they could go whitewater rafting. They would be parents with their children that are about 12 years and older. They would love the outdoors and nature. They would love to be active. The target audience would seek adventures and excitement by trying new things or experience whitewater rafting again. This site would meet the audiences needs by educating them on locations, gear, safety, and things they could do in addition to whitewater rafting in these areas. The site would be accessible through multiple sources such as on their mobile device and their desktop browser.
